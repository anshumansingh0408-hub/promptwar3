About CarbonWise

Empowering individuals with deterministic carbon logic and state-of-the-art AI recommendations to create a sustainable tomorrow.

🌍 1. What is CarbonWise?

CarbonWise is a climate-tech web application that calculates household monthly carbon footprint emissions and delivers personalized conversational green living advice through a context-aware AI chat assistant.

Our mission is to democratize carbon accounting, translating raw, confusing ecological data into simple, actionable habits. By combining a deterministic calculation engine with a state-of-the-art Large Language Model, we provide users with immediate, localized insights tailored specifically to their household habits.

🧮 2. How We Calculate

CarbonWise employs a deterministic calculation engine based on fixed emission coefficients. We evaluate four core categories of household emissions:

🚗 Transport Emissions

Calculated using mode-specific coefficients (e.g., petrol car, diesel car, electric scooter, public bus, metro) multiplied by daily distance travelled and scaled to monthly values.

⚡ Electricity Emissions

Evaluated based on consumed electricity units (kWh/month) multiplied by national grid emission factors.

🍽 Diet Lifecycle

Evaluated using average lifecycle emissions associated with dietary categories (Vegan, Vegetarian, or Non-Vegetarian).

♻️ Waste Decays

Calculated using weekly non-recycled landfill waste generation values and typical municipal solid waste decay factors.

📚 4. Primary Data Sources

To guarantee scientific accuracy, our calculation coefficients are sourced from standard global and national climate databases:

✔ IPCC AR6 Reports ✔ CEA India Grid ✔ World Health Org (WHO) ✔ UNEP Guidelines

⚠️ Disclaimer

Calculations provided by CarbonWise represent estimates of greenhouse gas emissions based on average regional and global coefficients. Actual personal emissions can vary depending on specific vehicle models, local power utility configurations, solar installations, and individual behavior. Please consult certified energy auditors or professional sustainability experts for precise audit calculations.
