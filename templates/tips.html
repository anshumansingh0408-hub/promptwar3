Transport

Difficulty: ●●○
  • Transition to public transit systems like the metro or city buses for daily commutes.
  • Organize carpools or ride-shares to maximize vehicle occupancy.
  • Consider switching to an electric vehicle (EV) or a hybrid for your next car.
  • Arrange to work from home 2 days/week to eliminate commuting emissions.
*Local context: Delhi/Mumbai Metro networks save millions of private-vehicle trips daily.*
CO2 Savings Potential 180 / 500 kg / month
⚡

Electricity

Difficulty: ●○○
  • Replace traditional incandescent bulbs with high-efficiency LED lights.
  • Look for BEE 5-star rating labels when buying home appliances.
  • Install rooftop solar panels to generate clean energy locally.
  • Unplug standby devices and power strips when they are not in use.
*Local context: BEE 5-star rated ACs consume up to 30% less electricity than 1-star models.*
CO2 Savings Potential 120 / 300 kg / month
🍽

Diet

Difficulty: ●●●
  • Reduce consumption of red meats, which require high-carbon production methods.
  • Prioritize locally sourced and seasonal ingredients to cut food transport miles.
  • Minimize kitchen waste by planning meals and storing food properly.
  • Adopt a fully plant-based or vegan menu at least 2 days every week.
*Local context: Traditional low-meat diets (dal, roti, vegetables) represent massive carbon savings.*
CO2 Savings Potential 80 / 200 kg / month
♻️

Waste

Difficulty: ●○○
  • Compost organic kitchen scraps to reduce methane release in landfill dump sites.
  • Refuse single-use plastics and carry durable reusable shopping bags.
  • Repair items before replacing them to extend their product lifespan.
  • Buy pre-owned or second-hand clothes, furniture, and electronics.
*Local context: Solid waste segregation is mandatory in many Indian cities under municipal laws.*
CO2 Savings Potential 40 / 100 kg / month
💧

Water

Difficulty: ●○○
  • Fix leaking taps, pipes, and toilets promptly to prevent resource waste.
  • Take shorter showers and install low-flow aerator nozzle attachments.
  • Incorporate rainwater harvesting setups for watering garden vegetation.
  • Install highly efficient drip irrigation layouts for domestic landscaping.
*Local context: Rooftop rainwater harvesting is highly recommended in water-scarce Indian metros.*
CO2 Savings Potential 30 / 50 kg / month
🛍

Shopping

Difficulty: ●●○
  • Buy fewer physical items and choose experiences over material possessions.
  • Invest in high-quality items that last longer, reducing purchase cycles.
  • Select sustainable brands with transparent eco-friendly supply chains.
  • Avoid fast-fashion labels and buy classic, long-lasting clothing items.
*Local context: Supporting local Indian weavers, handloom fabrics, or khadi cuts shipping emissions.*
CO2 Savings Potential 50 / 150 kg / month
